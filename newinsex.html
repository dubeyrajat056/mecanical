About PrimeTech India

Our Story

Established in 2020, PrimeTech India has rapidly grown to become a trusted name in the manufacturing and supply of precision components and fasteners. Our journey began with a vision to provide high-quality engineering solutions to industries across India and beyond.

With a dedicated team of 25 professionals and a 4500 sq ft manufacturing facility, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver products that meet the highest standards of quality and precision.

Our Mission

To be the preferred partner for precision components and fasteners by delivering superior quality products, exceptional customer service, and innovative solutions that exceed expectations.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized manufacturer of precision engineering components through continuous innovation, quality excellence, and sustainable business practices.
